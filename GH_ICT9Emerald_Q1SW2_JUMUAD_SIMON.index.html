Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin or Crisostomo Ibarra, is a "mestizo" who returned to the Philippines after studying in Europe. He is very kind, wise and noble like his father.
Maria Clara de los Santos Maria Clara de los Santos or Maria Clara is Crisostomo Ibarra's childhood friend. They were both seperated after Ibarra went to Europe to study while Maria Clara stayed in the Philippines and studied at the "beateryo"
Don Rafael Ibarra The father of Crisostomo Ibarra is Don Rafael Ibarra. He is a wealthy and well known person in San Diego. Some even consider him the most generous person in San Diego despite being the wealthiest
Don Santiago de los Santos Don Santiago de los Santos or Kapitan Tiago is the "Kapitan" in San Diego. Kapitan Tiago gained his wealth through trading Opium with Chinese. He doesn't consider himself a Filipino because he calls himself a Spaniard
Padre Damaso Verdolagas Padre Damaso was the former curate of San Diego before being replaced by Padre Salvi. Padre Damaso is an arrogant priest and he was the one who opposed the marriage of Crisostomo Ibarra and Maria Clara
