HOME SAFETY AND FIRE PREVENTION IN HOUSES

Ways to Prevent Fires in Households

Unplug devices not in use

Whenever we leave our homes, we should unplug all electric wires to prevent a problem we won't be able to fix. This can also prevent accidents that no one would be able to take care of in time

Do not smoke indoors

Smoking indoors can not only harm you but also your house as smoke can be trapped indoors without a ventilation system. We should also dispose of the smoke properly to prevent starting a fire

Frequently check electrical wires

We must frequently get our electricity checked to decrease risk of electric failure. Getting our electricty checked would let us be prepared and let us know when to replace our outlets and such.

Handle flammable gases with caution

Flammable gas should always be kept away from appliances such as stoves when not in use as even a small spark can cause a fire accident. We must only use flammable gas when we are sure there are no fire threats around.
