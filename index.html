Famous Dog Breeds

Siberian Husky Siberian Huskies are a very smart, active and gentle dog breed. Huskies are able to adapt to different living environments. Huskies get around with people which is why it is rather safe to have children around them. Onto their appearance, they have similar appearances to wolves like having erect ears, a thick coat and tail. They have characteristics like power, endurance and speed all in one.

Chihuahua According to sources, Chihuahuas are the smallest dog breed in the world. Their personality does not compare for their size though as you can get one with a big personality and a big heart, or a really feisty one. These dogs are able to learn on how to socialize, making them quirky pets suited for a small family. Another part about their appearance, aside from being small in size, they have tall, upright ears with large eyes to top it with pretty long tails. Despite their small body however, they have muscular bodies.

Toy Poodle Another small dog on our list are the poodles, the miniature ones. With their small size comes with speed making them natural athletes as they can run, jump and swim like other breeds. Poodles have very elegant and well proportioned body along with their curly hair all over the body. Most poodles are often seen with different types of haircuts which further reveal their long and fluffy fur.

Pug Pugs are small yet muscular dogs who are known for their loving identities. They are easy to train but are also good to play with, curl with and even nap with. They have such good personalities that most kids choose them as their favorite dog breed. Onto their appearance wecan see their compact, strong and thick bodies. On their face though we can see an almost flat nose, with really wrinkly face but it is still considered cute by many but take note that compared to other dog breeds, pugs have a more difficult time breathing.

Beagle Beagles are a dog breed that is very loyal and are very physically active. They are a happy breed but they also have a lot more good personalities like being gentle, affectionate, playful, etc. Beagles are also smart and they are good with memorizing patterns and certain scents which is why they don't get lost often. On their appearance, they have similar trates to those of hounds yet they still keep their sturdy and compact build.
